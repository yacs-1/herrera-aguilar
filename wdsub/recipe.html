Best Brownies

    PREP WORK

    • Wash your hands
    • Preheat the oven to 350 degrees
    • Gather ingredients

  1. Step One

    1. Brown 1/2 cup of unsalted butter
    2. Whisk together the butter and 1/2 cup of cocoa powder
    3. Set aside.

  2. Step Two

    1. Whisk together 2 large eggs, with 1 1/2 cup of brownsugar
    2. Pour in the ingredients from step one and mix together

  3. Step Three

    1. Mix in 2 cups of all-purpose flour
    2. Add in chocolate chips

  4. Step Four

    1. Pour Brownie mix into a 9x9 pan and add more chocoalate chips as well as drizzle caramel sauce on top of the mixture
    2. Bake for 15-20 minuites
    3. Let cool, and enjoy<3
